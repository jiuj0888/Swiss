Swiss Travel Pass Calculator | Compare Swiss Travel Pass, Half Fare Card & Jungfrau VIP

How to use Swiss Travel Pass Check (click to expand)
1) Enter travelers
- Start by entering the number of Adults (25+), Youth (16–25), Children (6–15), and Infants (0–5).
2) Select season
- Pick a travel date and the season is set automatically. If your trip is near the boundary, you can override it manually.
3) Choose passes / tickets
- Select only the passes or tickets you actually plan to use.
4) Choose regions & routes
- Expand a region and check only the routes you will actually take.
5) Enter route fares
- Search fares on SBB or the operator website, then enter Adult / Youth / Child / Infant fares separately.
- Routes fully covered by your selected pass are locked automatically as CHF 0.
6) Review the result
- Check the total on the left and the breakdown below to see which routes are free, discounted, or still need fare input.
Category guide

How to enter fares
  • Search your route fare on SBB or the operator website.
  • Enter fares separately for Adult / Youth / Child / Infant.
  • Make sure your entered fare already reflects one-way or round-trip, depending on how you plan to travel.
  • Routes fully covered by Swiss Travel Pass, Flex, or VIP are auto-locked.
