Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 17, 2026

Swiss Fare Check ("we," "our," or "the site") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information may be collected when you use this website, how it may be used, and what choices you have.

1. Who we are

Swiss Fare Check is an independent travel fare comparison website designed to help users compare Swiss travel passes and route fares more easily.

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact us at:

jiuj0888@gmail.com

2. Information we collect

We do not require account registration to use this site.

Depending on how you use the site, we may collect or process the following types of information:

  • Information you enter directly into the calculator, such as travel dates, passenger counts, selected passes, selected routes, and manually entered fares
  • Basic technical information automatically provided by your browser or device, such as browser type, device type, IP address, language settings, referring page, and approximate location based on IP
  • Usage and analytics data, such as page views, clicks, scroll activity, and general interaction patterns
  • Information stored locally in your browser, such as selected options and fare inputs, to help preserve your session and improve usability

3. How we use information

We may use information for the following purposes:

  • To operate and improve the calculator and related site features
  • To remember your recent selections and inputs on your device
  • To understand general usage trends and improve the user experience
  • To detect bugs, abuse, fraud, or technical issues
  • To respond to messages, feedback, or bug reports sent by email
  • To display advertising, where applicable, and to measure advertising performance

4. Local storage

This site may use browser local storage to save your recent calculator state, such as selected routes, entered fares, passenger counts, and preferences. This data stays on your device unless your browser clears it.

5. Cookies and similar technologies

This site may use cookies or similar technologies for:

  • Basic site functionality
  • Analytics
  • Remembering preferences
  • Advertising and ad measurement

If advertising is enabled in the future, third-party vendors such as Google may use cookies to serve ads based on a user’s prior visits to this site or other websites.

This site may use Google AdSense or other advertising services in the future.

10. Data retention

We retain personal information only for as long as reasonably necessary for the purposes described in this Privacy Policy, unless a longer retention period is required by law.
